10  * @section description Description
11  * Mk2_3phase_RFdatalog_temp.ino - Arduino program that maximizes the use of home photovoltaic production
12  * by monitoring energy consumption and diverting power to one or more resistive charge(s) when needed.
13  * In the absence of such a system, surplus energy flows away to the grid and is of no benefit to the PV-owner.
14  *
15  * @section history History
16  * __Issue 1 was released in January 2015.__
17  *
18  * This sketch provides continuous monitoring of real power on three phases.
19  * Surplus power is diverted to multiple loads in sequential order. A suitable
20  * output-stage is required for each load; this can be either triac-based, or a
21  * Solid State Relay.
22  *
23  * Datalogging of real power and Vrms is provided for each phase.
24  * The presence or absence of the RFM12B needs to be set at compile time
